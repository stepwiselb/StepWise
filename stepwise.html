What It Does
BUILT FOR
PEAK PERFORMANCE
👣

Gait Analysis

Tracks heel strike angle, gait phase, cadence, and ground contact time — giving you a complete biomechanical picture of how you move.

📊

Live Motion Metrics

Step count, cadence, and real-time movement data streamed directly from sensors embedded in the sole to your smartphone.

🧠

AI Coaching

The companion app analyzes your movement patterns and delivers personalized coaching tips to help you move better and avoid injury.

🚨

Fall Detection

Automatic fall detection sends instant emergency alerts to designated contacts — designed for athletes, elderly users, and rehab patients.

🤌

Gesture Controls

Customize foot gestures for hands-free interaction with the app — pause music, trigger actions, and more without touching your phone.

📱

Session History

Review every session in the companion app. Spot trends, track progress over time, and see how your movement improves week by week.

The Process
HOW IT
WORKS
01

Wear & Pair

StepWise sensors are embedded in the sole. Open the app, connect via Bluetooth, and you're ready to go in seconds.

02

Move Freely

Walk, run, or train. The system silently captures real-time biomechanical data including gait phase, cadence, and heel strike angle.

03

Get AI Coaching

The app analyzes your movement patterns and delivers personalized coaching insights to improve form and prevent injury.

04

Stay Safe

Fall detection runs in the background at all times, sending instant emergency alerts to your contacts if a fall is detected.
